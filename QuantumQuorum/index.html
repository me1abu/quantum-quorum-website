About the society

QUANTUM QUORUM is the official physics society of GTBIT founded with a vision to ignite curiosity and foster a passion of science among students .The society hosts events that teach the students about physics in a playful manner, the members of society aims to ignite curiosity among students for the subject by making various projects , launching newsletter, and giving a scope of research.
