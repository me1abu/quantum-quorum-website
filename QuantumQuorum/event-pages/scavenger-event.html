About Event

Event Overview

SCAVENGER WITHOUT HUNT was the inaugural event of Quantum Quorum, aiming to spark curiosity and excitement about physics. It blended fun with education, engaging participants in creative and interactive ways.

Event Highlights

  • 6 physics-based mini-games
  • A quiz featuring fictional and non-fictional science questions
  • Faculty and fresher participation
  • Fun, competition, and learning all in one experience

Participant Stats

  • 100+ participants from across all departments
  • Event structure: 6 physics-based mini-games + 1 finale quiz
  • Final quiz involved top 4 scoring teams
  • Faculty support from senior professors, including Dr. Daljeet Kaur
